Frequently asked

How do I start a match?

Open Doppio and tap Start for a quick match, or set up a named doubles match to enter player names. Award points by tapping each team's zone; tap Undo to take back an accidental point.

Can I resume a match later?

Yes. An in-progress match is saved automatically, so it survives closing or relaunching the app and can be resumed from the Home screen. When you close a match you'll be asked whether to keep it (resumable) or discard it.

How does Apple Watch scoring work?

When a match is open on your iPhone, the phone keeps the official score and your Apple Watch mirrors it — taps on the Watch are sent back to the phone, so the two never disagree. When no match is open on your phone, the Watch can score a match on its own. Make sure both apps are installed and your devices are paired.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays on your device — there's no account and no server. Deleting the app removes its match history and settings from that device. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Which languages are supported?

Doppio is available in English and Italian, following your device language.

Turning off haptics or voice

Open Settings inside the app to toggle haptics, spoken score announcements, server guidance, and side-change prompts.
