Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

The short version:

Doppio keeps your matches and settings on your device. There is no account, no server, no analytics, and no tracking. We don't collect your data.

Doppio (“the app”, “we”) is a padel score tracker for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch, published by Marco Sanguineti. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with information.

Information stored on your device

Doppio stores the following locally on your iPhone, iPad, or Apple Watch so it can run scoring and show your history:

  • Match date, time, and duration
  • Scoring format and rules you choose
  • Team or player names, if you choose to enter them
  • Point-by-point match events, the final result, and basic statistics
  • App preferences such as scoring format, haptics, voice announcements, server guidance, and side-change prompts

This information stays on your device. It is used only to run scoring, show your match history, generate result cards, and remember your preferences.

No account and no servers

Doppio does not require you to create an account. We do not operate a server for match storage, profiles, rankings, or remote sync. Your match history is not uploaded to us.

No analytics or advertising tracking

Doppio does not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs and does not track you across other apps or websites. It does not request access to your location, contacts, calendars, photos, microphone, camera, or advertising identifiers, and it does not integrate with HealthKit or read any fitness or medical data.

Apple Watch and iPhone sync

When a match is open on your iPhone, Doppio can mirror the score to your paired Apple Watch and send your taps back, so you can score from either device. This sync happens locally between your own paired devices using Apple's Watch Connectivity; the match state is not sent to any Doppio server.

Sharing match cards

If you choose to share a match result card, Doppio uses the standard Apple share sheet. Whatever destination you pick — Messages, WhatsApp, Mail, Instagram, or another app — handles that shared content under its own privacy practices.

TestFlight, the App Store, and crash reports

If you install Doppio through TestFlight or the App Store, Apple may provide us with crash reports, diagnostics, and tester feedback depending on your device settings and choices. We use that information only to fix bugs and improve the app. This data is handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy.

Device backups

Local Doppio data may be included in your Apple device or iCloud backups, depending on your device settings. Those backups are operated by Apple, not by Doppio.

Deleting your data

Deleting the app from a device deletes the Doppio match history and preferences stored on that device.

Children

Doppio is a general sports utility and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If Doppio later adds features such as analytics, iCloud sync, accounts, payments, or HealthKit, we will update this policy and the App Store privacy details before those features are released.
